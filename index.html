About Us

Founded in 2023 by “Luciosinferno”, Pesky Birds is a small game development studio creating engaging Minecraft Bedrock addons and DLC. What began as a hobby in 2017 grew into a dedicated studio, releasing custom gameplay experiences, skins, and textures enjoyed by players across MCPEDL and CurseForge. To date, we’ve released over projects, including add-ons, skin packs, and textures, amassing more than worldwide.
